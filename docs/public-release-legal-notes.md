# Public release legal notes for human review

These notes align public copy, Privacy, and Terms with the implemented Sovereign.OS product. They are not legal advice and must be reviewed by counsel before production release.

## Privacy coverage required

Sovereign.OS processes account information, email verification events, Turnstile audit events, raw Baseline onboarding inputs, reduced Baseline representations, location precision settings, current-condition context, invited people, relationship metadata, system membership, consent scopes, AI thread events, saved understandings, billing identifiers, export jobs, deletion jobs, operational logs, Cloudflare Worker/D1/AI Gateway processing, Stripe subscription processing, email delivery logs, retention state, and consent revocation.

## Terms coverage required

Terms must describe the product scope, non-diagnostic use, no medical/legal/crisis/therapeutic replacement, account verification, invited-person responsibilities, consent, acceptable use, Free and Sovereign+ subscription behavior, cancellation, voluntary support payments that do not grant entitlements, exports, deletion, platform availability, and contact process.
